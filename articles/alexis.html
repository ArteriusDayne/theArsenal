Wenger: Alexis Has An Extra Gear

Posted 11th Dec 2016 - Arsene Wenger says Arsenal are working hard to tie down Alexis Sanchez to a new long-term contract


Arsene Wenger says Arsenal are working hard to tie down Alexis Sanchez to a new long-term contract and that he still has time on his hands as negotiations continue.

The Chile international took his season’s goal tally to 10 with a decisive brace against Bournemouth on Saturday and completed his third full game in a row since returning from international duty where it was thought he may have a hamstring problem.

The 27-year-old’s man-of-the-match performance led to fulsome praise from the boss, who said afterwards:

“Even when he looks dead, he’s still alive and always finds the resources to do something special."
“He has that conditioning which allows him to always find an extra gear to be dangerous."
“It [his contract] is important. How important it is, I don’t know – we will not sort it tonight. We have some time in front of us.”
Asked whether the striker was growing more expensive to keep with every goal, Wenger also joked: “Well, you like us to spend money, so you should be happy!”
